Rituals of drawing

Media

Charcoal, ink, pastel, gesso on paper

These are experimental beginnings of a drawing series that focus on composition and form accumulated through resistance. Bodies of work from artists like Matthew Barney (DRAWING RESTRAINT 1987-present) and Heather Hansen (KINETIC DRAWING), mark-making becomes a performance, meditation training, and an imprint of accumulated time and captured impulses.

Dimensions of these works start from 50"x60" allowing plenty of room for full range motions- this makes it a challenge to capture and document the final outcome and its processes. But - more coming soon.
